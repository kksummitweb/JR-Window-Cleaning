About JR Window Cleaning

A young local business built on discipline and trust.

Jaden launched his window cleaning business in the summer of 2024 at just 14 years old, not for a quick paycheck, but to build something he could be proud of.

As an athlete, Jaden already understood what it takes to show up, push through, and perform at a high level, and he brought that same discipline, resilience, and work ethic to every job he takes on.

What began as a summer side hustle quickly transformed into a trusted local business built on professionalism, attention to detail, and a commitment to high quality results.

Jaden holds himself to a standard that goes beyond clean windows. It is about earning the trust of every customer, one job at a time.
